OUR TEAM

Absolute Robotics is a robotics team based at the Woodlands Secondary School in Mississauga, Ontario. We are currently made up of over 70 students, teachers, and mentors who are passionate about entrepreneurship, business, engineering, and technology!

Our members gain invaluable hands-on experiences in the world of engineering, problem solving and business. By competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), they develop key skills and competences that are crucial in our constantly evolving technological society.
