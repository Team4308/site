Subteams

Design

Design creates ideas to solve the problem given by FIRST Robotics through brainstorming, CAD, and prototyping.

Mechanical

Mechanical prototypes, constructes, tests and refines the physical mechanisms of the robot, as well as constructing arena pieces for testing.

Controls

Controls implements electronics, controls, and pneumatics systems for the robot. This includes wiring the robot and troubleshooting issues of the robot while also programming Java to create teleoperated commands for the drivers, and autonomous live updating systems for autonomous periods.

Marketing

Marketing organizes team finances and advertises the team. Activities often include organizing fundraisers and community events, and contacting sponsorships and mentors.

Media

Media focuses on promoting Absolute Robotics and the club's various events. Activites include collaborating with Marketing to help promote community events, attending competitions and taking photos, as well as designing merch and promo for the team!

Competition

The Competition subteam involves strategy and scouting. Strategy involves evaluating past games and real-time competition data in order to create winning game plans. Scouting is collecting and analyzing robot and game data of competing teams and recognizing strengths, weakness, strategies, and overall game play.
